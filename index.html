About Me

Developer & Design ?

Let’s start with the basics. My name is Romain, I am a 24 years old student following a Computer-Science engineering degree specialized in Human-Machine Interactions (HMI). So I am studying computer-science, but not only, I am also a very big design enthusiast. Design can mean a lot of things, so what do I mean by that ? Well I find a lot of interest into multiple “aesthetic” design related fields such as Architecture, Graphic Design, Interior Design etc. About two years ago I wondered what field were mixing both computer science and design. That’s when I heard about HMI and front-end which goes from User Experience to implementing sexy animations. I've tried since then to become a front-end developer by learning javascript, and it’s frameworks but also by learning about UI/UX design.
